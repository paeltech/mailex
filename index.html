About Us

We are a logistics and courier company.

Our journey started after noticing a gap in courier and logistics services, that's when we decided to incorporate MailEx to fix the gaps and to deliver excellent customer service to clients who sought after their parcels and cargo delivered to various locations in Tanzania.

Services

We provide state of the art logistics and courier services.

Our mission is to ensure excellent delivery of logistics and courier services efficient and state of the art customer care and consistent logistics services.

  • Pick up and delivery

    We provide pick up and delivery services by collecting, packaging and delivering parcels and small packages using our motorbikes and vans.

  • Brokerage services

    Using our MailEx app we provide brokerage services, that offer transportation of goods both over short and long distance by connecting customers with transportation companies.
